WE THINK OUTSIDE THE BOX

As leaders in retail logistics, we go above and beyond to provide unique and innovative solutions for our clients – whether you require our specialist White Glove expertise to reach the last mile, or essential Section 321 and Type 86 clearance to break international borders. Discover why the only way is SEKO.

Ecommerce

Combining global reach with local expertise, we fulfill over a million orders a day – providing invaluable access to multi-channel DCs around the globe, so you can expand into international territories and scale your business with just a single integration.

Learn more

Cross Border

As the first global logistics provider to initiate Type 86 clearance via air and ocean freight, we successfully transfer hundreds of thousands of parcels across international borders, while minimizing unnecessary customs charges and import delays on route.

Learn more

Global Returns

With just a single API integration, we are uniquely qualified to track your returns from end to end – improving customer visibility and communication via our fully branded and configurable portal, to ensure many happy returns around the world.

Learn more

Home Delivery

While heavyweight parcels are where we thrive, we excel in transferring packages of all shapes and sizes to the last mile – covering 75% of the US population within a 100-mile radius, so deliveries reach all your customers’ doors, together and on-time.
