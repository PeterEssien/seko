Dell EMC

Integrating global technology providers Dell EMC with our unique Transport Management System.

Formed in 2016, Dell EMC was created after worldwide tech titans Dell acquired leading IT provider EMC – a joint venture which now offers a vast catalogue of products and services across data storage, information security, virtualization, analytics and cloud computing.

The Challenge

With a fast paced evolving supply chain, DELL EMC needed a dynamic and configurable Transport Management System to replace their outdated SCI application and work as a single source platform – with the ability to scale.

 

The Solution

Providing a fully customizable Transport Management System, our SEKO TMS platform enabled Dell EMC to build out supply chain solutions and improve efficiency – so they could focus on other parts of their business, while SEKO took care of their outbound supply chain.

 

Benefits of the SEKO solution

  • Fast and cost-effective implementation of SEKO TMS
  • Single source of communication between logistics partners and vendors
  • Reduced overheads and increased lead times as a result of highly sophisticated routing engine – automating logistics decision-making for over 2,000 carrier lanes
  • Streamline logistics processes through dynamic integration with ERP tools, third party vendors and 3PL/4PL clients

 

The Result

SEKO TMS offered a single source of communication between Dell EMC’s logistics partners and vendors, alongside dynamic integration with ERP tools, third party vendors and 3PL/4PL clients – helping to streamline logistics processes and ensure the most efficient course of operation through real-time reporting and KPI metrics. By using a highly sophisticated routing engine to automate logistics decision-making for over 2,000 carrier lanners, Dell EMC was also able to reduce overheads and increase lead times.

 

 

"When we tendered for a replacement to our then outdated SCI application, we never envisaged that we would discover the solution within the freight forwarding industry. SEKO's TMS solution outshone all the competition because it was built by logisticans and thus covered all our requirements. It was speedily implemented at a cost that was both reasonable and recoverable. Our investment is measurable and improvements are readily and enthusiastically developed. We continue to work with the SEKO IT team to further progress the application and believe that this will be the engine that drives continuous improvement in our supply chain."

Senior Manager, IT, Dell EMC, Ireland
