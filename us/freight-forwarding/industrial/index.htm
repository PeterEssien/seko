Industrial and Manufacturing Logistics

SEKO supports organizations in the industrial sector with a suite of logistics services. Our expertise in heavyweight transportation and critical factory parts logistics helps shippers to overcome Global supply chain hurdles.

Our Global network of 120 locations across 40 countries, coupled with our dedicated and trained staff and best-in-class technology, provides dynamic logistics solutions to the industrial sector.

AEROSPACE AND AVIATION

SEKO's services for the Aerospace and Aviation industries provide Global aircraft manufacturing logistics support as well as both civilian and military aircraft operations and fulfillment logistics services. Regardless of the size of the engine or the same day transportation requirements you may have, we can help you manage your vendors and meet tight deadlines anywhere in the world.

MANUFACTURING SUPPORT

From pumps and valves to metal fabricating equipment, SEKO specializes in Global procurement via air, ocean and ground transportation for heavy machinery. We understand that, even though these items are heavy and sometimes oversized, you still need to meet your customer’s production schedules. Our worldwide network of offices can help you navigate through borders and into your client’s facility.

CRITICAL FACTORY PARTS REPLENISHMENT

When a factory goes down and needs a critical part replenished, you need a logistics partner that will answer the phone at all hours of the day, any time of year. Whether you require a personal courier or Next Flight Out air cargo services, SEKO is there for you.

CHEMICALS

From hazardous materials to regular drums of liquid compounds, we provide full service logistics and transportation support to the chemicals industry. We have trained staff at many locations ready to handle your chemical products.

BUILDING MATERIALS

Construction of homes, offices and factories continues to expand in almost every country. You need a reliable partner that operates worldwide, to help you gain access to new markets so you can expand your business. SEKO helps suppliers to the construction industry with fulfillment and transportation services.
 

WE SPECIALIZE IN SPECIAL HANDLING

We offer all sorts of project cargo services, including break bulk and RO/RO, and we deal in all manner of sizes and weights for specialized cargo.
