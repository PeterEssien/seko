our services

we make your mission, our mission

As your 'one stop' logistics provider, we can tailor a complete supply chain solution for your every requirement. Plus, our strong relationships with the leading air and ocean carriers ensure we can offer an extensive range of specialist shipping services, which few other logistics providers can.

Export compliance desk services

Including Internal Compliance Program (ICP) support to navigate export and import controls.

ITAR/EAR warehousing and distribution

As well as shipping dual use weapons of war, plus any other materials subject to export and import controls.

Global multimodal transportation

Military freight forwarding including air, ground and sea, even delivering to where there is yet any infrastructure.

Specialist shipping of ammunition & explosives

Plus drones and lithium batteries, which are professionally handled by our most experienced and qualified employees.

Aid and relief operations

Leveraging our partnerships and expertise to deliver urgent supplies to communities in crisis.

Military spec packaging & crating

Adhering to the strictest requirements when packing sensitive government freight, high-tech goods or specialist military equipment.

'On the ground' Site Survey Teams

Obtaining the necessary clearance for both cargo and personnel, so that no one is turned away at the gate.

expert Military Base Clearance

Guaranteeing that all personnel are credentialed, with equipment cleared for transport both on and off military installations.

PROJECT CARGO

Very unique, oversized shipments that require special handling are overseen by our Project Cargo group. Many of our global offices are specifically trained and equipped for logistics in military - moving your largest assets, safely and reliably, nearly anywhere in the world.
