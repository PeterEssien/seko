SEKO Next-Flight Out Shipping

Sometimes a next day service just isn’t fast enough – so for situations like these, or any other unforeseen emergency, we offer SEKO Next Flight Out, a time critical express air shipping service.

Maybe you have a last minute order, or a customer needs a replacement part for an assembly line that’s down. Or is ‘same day’ just the nature of your market?

SEKO Next Flight Out is available for shipments of any size, with no weight restrictions - and we’ll even do the packing for you if you need us to.

KEEPING YOU INFORMED

At SEKO we set ourselves apart from other logistics companies by always keeping our clients fully up-to-date on the status of their shipments. We’ll call you with location updates and even tell you the exact time when your shipment was delivered.

SUPPLY CHAIN VISIBILITY

Your air freight shipments are tracked by our 24 hours a day, 365 days a year client service and communications center. At any moment, we can trace the exact location of your shipment, anywhere in the world. And you can also track and trace your freight online.

SATURDAY AND SUNDAY

Since emergencies can happen anytime, we are also available on Saturdays and Sundays, and every holiday.
