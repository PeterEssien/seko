Finished Goods Installation

We support custom technical installations with experienced and highly-skilled White Glove professionals, as well as strict adherence to SOPs. Our single POC coordinates the project team, collaborating with your on-site technicians and site contacts to expertly handle the complex and time-sensitive set-up of your fixtures and displays, medical devices and high-tech equipment.

WE FINISH THE JOB

We navigate the last mile of your finished goods with skill, speed and compliance - employing White Glove specialists to successfully deploy your apparatus, alongside a vast network of logistics coordinators, program managers and inventory control teams to ensure it arrives on time.

COMMUNICATION IS KEY

At SEKO, we seamlessly coordinate new installations, planning and customer support with all parties, including field technicians, site contacts and building management – providing a detailed checklist and feedback process along every phase of the asset’s journey, so everyone can stay informed on the status of their finished goods.

SPARE PARTS LOGISTICS

We provide a full suite of customizable, high-standard spare parts logistics services, specializing in time-definite, White Glove transportation and logistics. Operating in more than 100 forward stocking locations (FSLs) across the US, we also have spare parts logistics centers in core locations across all continents – offering key services such as customs brokerage, installation, fiscal representation, quality control inspections and default testing.

Our award-winning technology platform MySEKO empowers our clients to instantly track their finished goods, as well as schedule bookings and installations using the most cost-effective routes and carriers.

Key Features of MySEKO

  • Robust Global search capacity
  • Detailed order management visibility
  • Collects data for financial and operational KPI analysis
  • Multi-language support
  • Global calendar and schedule maintenance
  • Efficient load planning capability
