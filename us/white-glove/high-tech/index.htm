High-Tech Logistics for Technology Sector

SEKO delivers secure, customized logistics solutions for the high-tech, telecommunications and consumer electronics industries. We provide speed to market for your high value electronics and hardware, with the care and White Glove service that you demand.

From Global air freight supply chains to highly technical assembly, distribution and time-definite deliveries, SEKO offers a suite of vital services for the High-Tech sector.

Retail Distribution for Consumer Electronics

We provide end-to-end supply chain solutions for the consumer electronics industry. From inbound air and ocean consolidation solutions, DC bypass programs, eCommerce fulfillment and retail store distribution, SEKO is your outsourced logistics partner.

POS Equipment Distribution and Replenishment

Complex paperwork, language difficulties or cultural barriers are all a thing of the past, as SEKO whisks your shipment from the dock, through customs and onto a truck for delivery to its final destination. We handle everything seamlessly.

High-Tech Logistics Management with SEKO MedTec

SEKO MedTec has developed a highly specialized logistics solution focused exclusively on the high-tech and medical device industries. SEKO MedTec provides dedicated program management for demonstration equipment, finished goods and returns, as well as, contract logistics and fulfillment services. 

Our engineering, transportation and software expertise is backed up by our control towers, knowledgeable staff and shared facilities - with clean rooms around the world for your high-tech hardware and equipment. We also offer warehousing, relocation and White Glove installation support for your servers, hardware and telecommunication equipment.

Inbound Supplier Management

We facilitate more streamlined supply chains by helping you to measure lead times, fulfillment and shipment information from your overseas and local vendors. When you need to keep your production line on schedule, with no delays, you can count on SEKO to help manage your suppliers effectively.

Contract Manufacturing Support

SEKO provides fulfillment solutions and third party warehousing for electronic component manufacturers in Asia, Europe and North America. With our data integration services, we can also communicate with your ERP systems; giving you visibility of your orders in real time, as they come through. 

Reverse Logistics

For electronics, hardware and equipment that needs to be replenished or replaced, we offer full removal & returns management programs anywhere in the world. In addition, we also provide environmentally friendly recycling options for high-tech equipment, with full documentation and support.

We Specialize in Special Handling

SEKO offers all sorts of project cargo services, including break bulk and RO/RO. We also deal in all manner of sizes and weights for specialized cargo, including server racks and displays.

HIGHLY EXPERIENCED INTEGRATED RACK LOGISTICS SOLUTIONS

Delivering valuable and sensitive equipment into some of the largest data centers around the globe is a precision operation from start to finish. Discover how we can ensure the safe White Glove handling of your integrated server racks, with zero chance of failure.
