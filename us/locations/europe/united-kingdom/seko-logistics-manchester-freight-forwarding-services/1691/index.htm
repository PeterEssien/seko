SEKO Logistics Manchester: Specialist Freight Forwarding Services

From value-added freight forwarding and 4PL bespoke designed TMS solutions, to international fulfilment and ecommerce, SEKO Manchester is equipped to meet your every logistics demand, on a global scale.

FACILITATING LIFE IN THE FAST CHAIN

Responding to your ever-changing supply chain, SEKO Manchester offers a unique infrastructure of highly experienced staff and state-of-the-art facilities, to support a diverse mix of industry sectors - including ecommerce, manufacturing, automotive, fashion, sports science and many more.

If you would like to discuss how we can help transform your operations, please get in touch.

A SPECTRUM OF SPECIALISED SERVICES

Our diverse client portfolio has provided us with direct experience in a number of different industries, enabling us to enrich our service offering, as well as action original and often unexpected solutions.

  • Global supply chain management
  • Value-added freight forwarding
  • Ecommerce fulfilment
  • Customs brokerage
  • Multimodal transport solutions
  • Bespoke designed IT suite – including our PO Management tool, TMS and WMS systems
