SEKO Logistics Egham: Global Cross-Border Logistics Specialists

Supporting all outbound and inbound logistics requirements, we ship everything from parcels to pallets, and beyond – taking care of our business-to-business and business-to-consumer clients, spanning ecommerce and freight logistics.

GLOBAL REACH STARTS HERE

Our SEKO Heathrow facility specialises in fast turnaround sortation and containerisation for outbound shipping – by road to Europe, and by air to the rest of world.

For inbound into the UK, we also manage import clearance and declarations, cross-dock and final mile delivery, with our HMRC and AEO approved in-house brokerage team not only ensuring that data is compliant, but clearance is smooth and expedited too. What's more, our close relationship with the airlines enables us to ensure capacity and optimum routing for global clients like Amazon, The Hut Group and Beauty Bay, so they can continue to successfully scale their business across borders. 

 

To discover what we can do for your business, please get in touch today.

SERVICES WHICH GO THE EXTRA MILE

Utilising SEKO OmniParcel for tracked ecommerce delivery and returns, as well as CargoWise for freight forwarding and tracking, our state-of-the-art technology platforms enhance the quality of our services. 

  • B2B freight forwarding
  • Outbound and inbound customs clearance
  • Parcel sortation and routing to destinations globally
  • Screening and prep for onward shipping
  • Bonded ETSF (External Temporary Storage Facility)
  • Retrieval from airlines
  • Inbound cross-dock, via air and road
  • T1 discharge
  • Returns consolidation and sortation, consumer returns and RTS

Why Choose SEKO HEATHROW?

Discover what makes our world-class SEKO Heathrow facility essential to our clients' success. 

ACCESSIBILITY

Located next to London Heathrow, so we can hand over and retrieve goods from the airlines daily.

COMPLIANCE

HMRC and AEO approved for total adherence to regulations.

SECURITY

Our facility is fully secure, with CCTV coverage and a number of expert safety measures.

FLEXIBILITY

Our team is cross-skilled to deal with any outbound and inbound shipping requirement.

 

STRATEGICALLY LOCATED

Only a short distance from the UK’s largest air hub, SEKO Heathrow provides total global reach alongside local expertise – facilitating the efficient exchange of goods from the airlines.
