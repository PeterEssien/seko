SEKO Logistics Milton Keynes: Specialist eCommerce Fulfilment Services

Shipping business to business, business to consumer and direct to consumer orders to our clients’ customers - wherever they are.

A GLOBAL GATEWAY TO YOUR CUSTOMERS

Our Milton Keynes facility is designed to support a range of industries and more importantly, the expansion of your business. From cross border parcel shipping to kitting and personalisation, we specialise in Global multi-channel solutions for a range of clients, including Virgin Atlantic, Mous, Manscaped and Castore.

If you would like to discuss how we can help transform your operations, please get in touch.

A SPECTRUM OF SPECIALISED SERVICES

Each of our shared user sites around the world operates on the same technology platform, providing order fulfilment services for brands and retailers. Elements of the service include:

  • Pallet, pick face and hanging storage
  • Pick and pack for B2B, B2C and D2C
  • Luxury and gift wrapping
  • Kitting
  • Embroidery
  • Printing apparel and accessories
  • Embossing and engraving
  • Domestic and cross border shipping
  • Real time inventory visibility
  • Batch control
  • Returns processing

Why Choose SEKO MILTON KEYNES?

ACCESSIBILITY

Milton Keynes is on the UK’s main arterial motorway, the M1 - close to all of the major carrier hubs and pallet networks.

ADAPTABILITY

Supporting our clients' growth by developing new services to meet objective.

AVAILABILITY

A wide range of pallet, pick face and hanging garment storage, as well as processing and production areas.

EFFICIENCY

Transfer of freight is improved with the support of 12 dock doors and 2 drive-in ramps.

SECURITY

Our warehouse is fully secured with hi-res cameras and electronic entry.

WMS TECHNOLOGY

SEKO’s 360 technology gives you full visibility of your inventory and all activities.
