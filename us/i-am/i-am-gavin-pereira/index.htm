Hi, I am...Gavin!

As Customs Manager for Brokerage (Customs) and Warehouse at SEKO South Africa, I am responsible for ensuring SEKO is compliant with all customs rules and regulations, correct duties are paid to customs, and that bond stores in warehouse are run correctly within regulations in South Africa.

TELL US A BIT ABOUT YOURSELF...

My name is Gavin Pereira – but you can call me Captain Corona!

As Customs Manager for Brokerage (Customs) and Warehouse at SEKO South Africa, I am responsible for:

  • Leading the Brokerage department who scrutinizes import/export documentation and then process the customs declarations
  • Managing the stock in bond store and arrange release from store when required through customs
  • Liaising directly with customers on complex issues
  • Ensuring my team are motivated

What is a typical day in the life?

EVERY DAY STARTS WITH...

A strong coffee! I’m the first person to arrive in the morning so I am in charge of opening the offices. Once my laptop is up and running, I then start checking on outstanding customs clearances and queries from the day before while enjoying the morning sunshine in my office with my cup of coffee.

PHEW, TIME FOR A BREAK...

On the menu today is Pizza! – Followed by an afternoon snack of Biltong or Dry Wors.

No day is complete without…

  • Checking that my staff are in control of the workflow on our dashboard the controllers from other departments are pushing
  • Ensuring any urgent matters in the day were addressed

I love being a part of Warehouse!

The management and staff in our office to start with are just so awesome and wonderful people to work with. Secondly, the fact that every day brings new challenges in this industry. After 27 years (9 with SEKO), I’m continually learning things.
