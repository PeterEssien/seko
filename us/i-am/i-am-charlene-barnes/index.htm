Hi, I am...Charlene!

As Head of Project Management for CSG at SEKO UK, I manage the organization to any implementations or development projects SEKO is doing internally. My job is to keep everyone on a strict deadline. I also work with the OMNI team to onboard eCommerce clients within fulfilment out of Milton Keynes.

TELL US A BIT ABOUT YOURSELF...

My name is Charlene Barnes – but you can call me The Task Manager!

As Head of Project Management for CSG at SEKO UK, I am responsible for:

  • Overseeing the organization to any implementations or development projects
  • Working with OMNI team to onboard eCommerce clients
  • Making sure deadlines are met

What is a typical day in the life?

EVERY DAY STARTS WITH...

Grabbing a cup of coffee! From there, I would check in with the team, as it is good to get a feel of what people are working on and what is happening.

PHEW, TIME FOR A BREAK...

On the menu today is Macro Meals! – Followed by an afternoon snack of cashews.

No day is complete without…

  • Conducting meeting to run through projects
  • Planning how to fix and succeed in certain fields & aspects
  • Getting updated on the flow and workload of the team

I love being a part of CSG!

I love my role at SEKO because it is very interesting, and every day is different. I get to see new faces, learn new products, and learn new processes. I also get the benefit of occasionally working with the same team members or clients further down the line.
