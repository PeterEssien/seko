Hi, I am...Alexandra!

As a Team Manager at SEKO Milton Keynes Distribution Centre, I am responsible for keeping the warehouse team on track with orders and fulfillment requests. Whether that be training or supporting individuals on their day-to-day tasks, I’m there! Discover what an average day looks like...

TELL US A BIT ABOUT YOURSELF...

My name is Alexandra Moldovan– but you can call me Busy Bee!

As a Team Manager at Milton Keynes Distribution Centre, I am responsible for:

  • Implementing strategies towards team members, delegating tasks and strengthening my teams skills or development throughout the MK DC

  • Making sure my team is motived and guided for all tasks at hand

  • Ensuring all team members are supported and have the resources to complete any and all tasks
  • Making sure all my team members are collaborating, working together effectively and efficiently getting orders processed, to reach our overall MK DC goals!

What is a typical day in the life?

EVERY DAY STARTS WITH... 

  • Checking the system to organize and prioritise my tasks for the day
  • Check system reports and reply to urgent emails
  • Check in with operations managers so we know what to expect throughout the day
  • Making sure my team is ready for the day with a quick briefing 

PHEW, TIME FOR A BREAK...

Which means I get to munch on my chicken with rice and veggies! Of course, I might sneak in a chocolate hob nob when no one is looking…

No day is complete without…

  • Making sure my staff and team know that they are appreciated for all the hard work they put in, especially after a long day. Working at the MK DC means you are always on the go. So the fact that I get to show how much this team means to me, but also SEKO as a whole, is important to amplify every day!

I love being a part of MK DC!

Because I love the people I work with! My bosses are super supportive and have allowed me to grow within my role, as they constantly believe in me to achieve any goals I set. Not only have they pushed and allowed me to develop a number of skills throughout my time here, but it is amazing to see how far I have come since joining SEKO!
